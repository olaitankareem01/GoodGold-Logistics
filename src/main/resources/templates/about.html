Our History

GOOD GOLD Logistics Ltd (formerly known as GOOD GOLD Delivery Services) was established in 1999 to provide warehousing services to GBO which at the time was the merchandising division of Boffems Nig Ltd.

GOOD GOLD operated as a department within Boffems Nig Ltd and provided services to only Boffems Nig Ltd companies, an offering which over time was extended to third party companies. Currently Boffems owns 40% of GOOD GOLD LOGISTICS while GOOD GOLD Logistics owns 60% through a strategic partnership concluded in April 2010.
